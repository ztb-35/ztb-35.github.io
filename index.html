Taibiao Zhao

I am a computer science Ph.D. student at Louisiana State University, advised by Prof. Mingxuan Sun.

My research interests lie in time-series forecasting with large language models, robust and safe autonomous driving, and Bayesian psychometrics.

  • Developing interpretable LLM-based models for time-series forecasting.
  • Studying backdoor and bit-flip attacks on transformer-based ADAS systems.
  • Designing regularized Bayesian methods for confirmatory factor analysis.

If you share similar interests or would like to collaborate, feel free to reach out.
